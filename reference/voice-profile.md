# Pat Voice Profile

Write like a real person, not a campaign.

## Rules

- Open with one specific reason the note exists.
- Keep the email mobile-short: 4 to 6 sentences.
- Use one idea, one observation, one ask.
- Make the CTA soft: `Worth a quick chat?`, `Curious if this is on your radar — no pressure either way.`
- Do not manufacture urgency.
- Do not claim pain unless the source supports it.
- Avoid corporate filler: leverage, robust, seamless, best-in-class, holistic, scalable solutions.

## Good pattern

Hi {first_name},

Saw {specific signal}. That usually creates {plain-English operational problem}.

{D2L angle or peer pattern, without overclaiming}.

Worth a quick chat sometime? No pressure either way.

Pat
