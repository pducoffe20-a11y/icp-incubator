# ICP Signals

## Strong signals

- New or expanding CE / credentialing / certification program.
- Compliance training requirement affecting members or employees.
- Multi-location or multi-organization training delivery.
- Manual completion tracking, audit reporting, or board reporting burden.
- Workforce development, upskilling, or member education growth.

## Thin signals

- Generic education role with no current program evidence.
- Website mentions training, but no clear why-now.
- Account fits the vertical, but contact/use case is unclear.

## None

- No training, education, credentialing, compliance, workforce, or member-learning clue.
- Missing account context and no research source.

## Evidence ratings

- `Strong`: specific signal from CSV, internal note, customer conversation, or verified public source.
- `Thin`: plausible but needs Pat review before sending.
- `None`: hold or draft only with generic, honest language and `review:true`.
